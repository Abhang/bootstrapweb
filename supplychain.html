The ASMS Approach

Assessment Strategy Development Process Optimization Implementation Planning Execution and Monitoring Continuous Improvement
Evaluate existing supply chain processes and identify areas for improvement. Collect data on inventory management, logistics, and supplier relationships.
Formulate a targeted supply chain strategy to address identified challenges. Emphasize improvements in cost efficiency, lead times, and overall effectiveness.
Identify and implement process improvements within the supply chain. Streamline workflows and enhance efficiency without a primary focus on technology.
Develop a clear plan for executing process improvements. Allocate resources effectively and establish realistic timelines.
Implement the optimized processes according to the established plan. Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) to track the impact of changes.
Regularly assess the performance of the optimized processes. Make ongoing adjustments based on feedback and changing business requirements.
